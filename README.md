# ScanNut

**ScanNut** is a comprehensive Flutter application for **Pet Health, Nutrition Management, and Support Partners**. It leverages AI to provide detailed biological profiles, nutritional analysis, and eco-friendly PDF reports.

## Key Features

### üêæ Pet Management & Health
- **Detailed Profiles**: Manage pet identity, including breed analysis (temperament, care), sex, microchip, and photos.
- **Medical History**: Track vaccines, medications, surgeries, veterinary visits, **baths, and grooming**.
- **Wound Analysis**: AI-powered wound analysis with photo history and severity assessment.
- **Gallery & Documents**: Store medical documents (PDFs) and gallery images.

### ü•ó Nutrition & AI Analysis
- **Food Analysis**: Analyze food images to get nutritional insights (Kcal, ingredients).
- **Weekly Meal Plan**: comprehensive meal plans with detailed daily schedules and nutritional goals.
- **Eco-Friendly PDF Reports**: Generate ink-saving PDF reports containing **ALL** data (Identity, Health, Nutrition, Gallery, Partners).
  - *New*: Minimalist design with no background colors to save ink.
  - *New*: Full data mapping (nothing is left out).

### ü§ù Partner Hub (Rede de Apoio)
- **Partner Registry**: Manage support partners (vets, clinics, sitters).
- **Radar Explorer**: Find nearby partners with configurable search radius.
- **Reports**: Generate partner summaries or detailed reports sorted by category.
