Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

NOTICE: ScanNut uses Artificial Intelligence. Generated results for Plants, Pets, and Food are for informational purposes only and do not replace professional advice.

1. Information Collection

ScanNut requests access to your camera and photo gallery strictly to process the images you choose to analyze. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless voluntarily provided.

2. Data Processing and AI

Images are securely transmitted via HTTPS to AI service providers (Google Gemini) for analysis. We do not store your images on our permanent servers or sell them to third parties.

3. Local Storage & Security

Your records (Food history, Pet medical records, Plant scans) are stored locally on your device using AES-256 military-grade encryption. Security keys are managed by the operating system (Secure Storage/Keystore).

4. Permissions

  • Camera: Used to capture images of food, plants, and pet health for AI screening.
  • Microphone: Used for optional voice-to-text transcription in clinical dossiers.
  • Location: Used for the "Partner Radar" feature to find nearby clinics and shops.

5. Subscriptions & Payments

ScanNut offers premium subscriptions managed by RevenueCat via Google Play. We do not process or store payment details.

6. Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all your data directly within the app under "Settings > Danger Zone". Clearing app data without a backup will result in permanent loss.

7. Backup Responsibility

The maintenance and security of exported local backup files (.scannut) are the sole responsibility of the user.
