Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

NOTICE: ScanNut uses Artificial Intelligence. Generated results for Plants, Pets, Food, and Vehicles are for informational purposes only and do not replace professional advice.

1. Information Collection

ScanNut requests access to your camera and photo gallery strictly to process the images you choose to analyze. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) unless voluntarily provided.

2. Data Processing and AI

Images are securely transmitted via HTTPS to AI service providers (such as Google Gemini) for analysis. We do not store your images on our permanent servers or sell them to third parties.

3. Local Storage

Your scan history is stored locally on your device using Hive technology. You can delete all your records at any time through the "Clear History" option in the app.

4. Permissions

  • Camera: Used to capture photos of plants, pets, food, and vehicles.
  • Gallery: Used to upload existing images for analysis.

5. Subscriptions & Payments

ScanNut offers premium subscriptions managed by RevenueCat via Google Play. We do not process or store payment details.

6. Location (Optional)

For the "Nearby Partners" feature, we may request approximate location access. This data is not tracked or stored.

7. Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all your data and account directly within the app under "Settings > Danger Zone".
